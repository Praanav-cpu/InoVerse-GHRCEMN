Our Mission

Inoverse Ideathon aims to foster innovation in the Web3 space by bringing together the brightest minds in Kerala. We believe in the power of decentralized technologies to transform industries and create new opportunities.

Our hackathon provides a platform for developers, designers, and entrepreneurs to collaborate, learn, and build cutting-edge solutions that leverage blockchain technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the Inoverse Ideathon?

Anyone with an interest in Web3 technologies can participate! Whether you're a student, professional, or enthusiast, as long as you're 18 or older, you're welcome to join.

Do I need prior experience with blockchain?

While some familiarity with blockchain concepts is helpful, we welcome participants of all skill levels. We'll have workshops and mentors to help you get started.

How many people can be in a team?

Teams can consist of 2-4 members. You can also register individually, and we'll help you find teammates during the team formation phase.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in the Inoverse Ideathon is completely free of charge!
